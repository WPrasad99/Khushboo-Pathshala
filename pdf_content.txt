1. Executive Summary 
The Khushboo Scholar Learning & Engagement Dashboard  is a centralized digital 
platform envisioned for the Cybage Khushboo Scholarship Program. The objective of this 
platform is to enhance student learning, engagement, tracking, and mentor?mentee 
collaboration through a single, structured system. 
This dashboard aims to replace fragmented tools such as WhatsApp messages, Excel 
sheets, and manual tracking processes with a scalable, transparent, and role-based 
solution.
 
2. Background & Problem Statement 
Currently, multiple activities under the Khushboo Scholarship Program?such as learning 
session updates, attendance tracking, resource sharing, and mentor interactions?are 
managed across disconnected platforms. This results in: 
? Manual and error-prone attendance processes 
? Limited visibility into student engagement and learning continuity 
? Communication overload via WhatsApp groups 
? Difficulty in tracking holistic student progress 
There is a clear need for a single integrated platform  that centralizes learning, 
communication, and reporting. 
 

3. Project Objectives 
The primary objectives of this project are to: 
? Create a centralized learning and engagement platform for Khushboo scholars 
? Enable automatic and transparent attendance tracking 
? Improve visibility into session engagement and learning continuity 
? Facilitate structured mentor?mentee interactions 
? Provide a single source of truth for announcements and updates 
? Generate meaningful progress and engagement reports 
 
4. Stakeholders 
Stakeholder Role 
Students (Khushboo Scholars) Primary users of the learning 
platform 
Mentors Learning facilitators and 
progress reviewers 
Cybage CSR/Admin Team Program oversight and 
reporting 
Hackathon Review Panel Evaluation and feedback 
 
5. User Roles & Access Control 
5.1 Student 
? Access learning resources and session videos 
? Automatic attendance via session completion 
? View personal dashboard (attendance, achievements, updates) 
? Participate in Q&A discussion forum 
? View mentor feedback and progress reports 
5.2 Mentor 
? Upload learning resources and session videos 
? Upload offline attendance using CSV files 
? Track student engagement and session continuity 
? Manage mentor?mentee meetings and feedback 
 

5.3 Admin (CSR Team) 
? Manage users and roles 
? Publish announcements and event updates 
? Monitor platform usage and analytics 
? View consolidated student and mentor reports 
 
6. Functional Requirements 
6.1 Learning Resources Management 
? Centralized repository for free educational resources 
? Resource categories: 
o Technical Skills 
o Soft Skills 
o Career Guidance 
? Upload permissions for Admin/Mentor 
? Read-only access for Students 
 
6.2 Learning Sessions & Video Tracking 
? Upload recorded learning session videos 
? Track: 
o Session start and end 
o Video completion percentage 
o Drop-off point 
? Attendance automatically marked when completion ? 80% 
 
6.3 Attendance Management 
Online Sessions:  - Auto attendance based on video completion logic 
Offline Sessions:  - Mentor uploads attendance via CSV - System validates and maps data 
to student records - Attendance reflected on student dashboards 
 
6.4 Student Dashboard 
Each student will have a personalized dashboard displaying: - Attendance percentage - 
Completed learning sessions - Achievements / badges (dummy data) - Placement updates 
(dummy data) - Announcements and alerts 

6.5 Announcements & Communication 
? Platform-based announcements 
? Session and event notifications 
? Acts as a structured replacement for WhatsApp communication 
 
6.6 Q&A / Discussion Forum 
? Access restricted to Khushboo beneficiaries 
? Topic-based discussion threads 
? Question posting and replies 
 
6.7 Mentor?Mentee Program 
Mentor?Mentee Mapping  - Capture mentor and mentee profiles - Assign mentors to 
students 
Meeting Feedback  - Meeting date and duration - Discussion summary - Feedback and 
remarks 
Progress Tracking  - Individual student progress reports - Mentor observations and 
engagement indicators 
 
6.8 Reports & Analytics 
? Attendance and participation reports 
? Session engagement insights 
? Mentor?mentee interaction summaries 
 
7. Non-Functional Requirements 
? Dummy data usage only 
? Secure authentication and role-based access 
? Scalable architecture (prototype-level) 
? Clean, intuitive, and responsive UI 
 
 
 

8. Assumptions & Constraints 
? No real student or mentor data will be used

This prototype is developed strictly for academic and hackathon purposes. All data used

legal and compliance expectations.
